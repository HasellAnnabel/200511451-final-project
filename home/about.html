Our History

We are an online store founded by 3 tea-loving sisters. We have created this store to share with you the great tea journey. For this adventure, we have traveled all over Asia in search of the best possible tea blends and flavors.

Hao Fresh, founded in 2022, offers us combinations of black tea, bubble tea, and milk tea. We also offer home delivery services, making enjoying your tea a convenient and pleasant experience.

We invite you to join our family and enjoy our services. If you give us your trust we can make this shop even bigger and make more people can also enjoy this experience.
